About Us

Poxly is a cutting-edge application designed to help users detect pox cases and stay informed about related outbreaks. Our mission is to leverage technology to promote public health and awareness.

We provide up-to-date data on local cases, deliver the latest monkeypox news, and offer detailed documentation for healthcare professionals and the public.

Our team is committed to ensuring the accuracy and reliability of the information provided through our platform.
